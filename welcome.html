Welcome to NetPass

Never manually log into your LNMIIT WiFi again. NetPass handles it — automatically, every time.

One-time credential setup
Auto-login every 4 hours
Works on macOS & Windows

Step 1 — Save Credentials

Click the NetPass icon in your Chrome toolbar and enter your LNMIIT WiFi username & password.

1
Click the NetPass icon in Chrome toolbar
2
>Enter your WiFi username and password
3
Click Save Credentials

Your credentials are stored locally in your browser. They are never sent anywhere.

Step 2 — Auto-Login

The extension automatically checks your WiFi connection every 4 hours. If you're disconnected, it opens the login portal and fills your credentials.

Checks internet connectivity
Opens WiFi portal if needed
Fills credentials & logs in
Closes the tab automatically

Step 3 — Auto-Start (Optional)

Want Chrome to open automatically when you log in? Run the script for your OS:

Open Terminal and run:

cd ~/Downloads/Auto-Connect-main ./install_launch_agent.sh

Open PowerShell (or double-click the file) and run:

cd ~\Downloads\Auto-Connect-main .\install_task_scheduler.bat

This ensures Chrome starts on login so the extension stays active.
